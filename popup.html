Basics:

Press cmd + shift + p to open the extension when using your browser. Your prompts will be available when viewing any site

You can add a new prompt to your library by typing or pasting it into the “Enter new prompt” dialogue, then hitting enter or clicking the “Add Prompt” button

Use the up and down keys to navigate through the prompts in your library

Hit enter to select a prompt

Press shift + enter to copy all your selected prompts to your clipboard, automatically closing the extension window. Alternatively you can click the “Copy Selected Prompts” button at the bottom

Tags:

You can add tags to prompts for easy access to prompts that share a common trait. For example, “Writing”, “Coding”, or “Therapy”.

You can add tags by clicking the “Edit” button on one of your prompts

Click the “Filter Prompts” button to select a tag to show
